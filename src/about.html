At Vakeel Babu, we believe that legal understanding should not be a privilege—it should be accessible, clear, and empowering for all.

As a consultancy founded by a law graduate, we provide non-litigious legal support, focusing on documentation, compliance guidance, and legal awareness. Whether you're a small business, startup, student, or individual, our mission is to simplify legal processes and help you make informed decisions with confidence.

We do not represent clients in court - but we do offer clarity, structure, and trusted support in navigating legal paperwork, procedures, and compliance frameworks.

From drafting professional agreements to conducting legal literacy workshops, we stand with you as partners in prevention, preparation, and progress. Our goal is not just to assist, but to educate - bridging the gap between everyday people and everyday law.

What's the history or story behind your legal consultancy service?


The agency wants to:

  • Make legal advice more approachable and can guide people through processes such as filing claims, representing themselves in small cases, or understanding court procedures.

  • Ensure that legal documents such as contracts, wills, and leases are properly drafted to avoid future legal issues.

  • Advice on the best course of action, whether that involves negotiation, mediation, or pursuing legal action in court.

  • Provide advice on contracts, business structures, and other legal matters that could otherwise result in costly litigation.

  • Help people interpret complex laws and apply them to specific situations.

  • Ensure that individuals and businesses are aware of their rights and help protect them from potential violations.
